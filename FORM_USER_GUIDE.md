## Troubleshooting

### Issue: Form Not Loading

**Solution**:
- Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
- Clear browser cache and reload
- Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Check internet connection
- JavaScript must be enabled

### Issue: Cannot Upload Files

**Solution**:
- File must be JPG, PNG, GIF, or WebP format
- Maximum file size is typically 5-10 MB
- Try different image format
- Ensure image is clear and readable
- Check file permissions

### Issue: Email Validation Error

**Solution**:
- Ensure email format: `name@domain.com`
- No spaces in email address
- Check "Confirm Email" matches "Email Address" exactly
- Common error: Extra space at beginning/end

### Issue: Phone/Fax Numbers Showing Error

**Solution**:
- Only numbers allowed (0-9)
- Can include: + - ( ) and spaces
- Remove any letters or special characters
- Try international format: +1-234-567-8900

### Issue: Date Validation Error

**Passport Issue Date Error**:
- You selected a future date
- Must be a date in the past or today
- Check calendar year is correct

**Passport Expiry Date Error**:
- You selected a past date (expired)
- Passport must be valid (expiry date in future)
- Check calendar year is correct

### Issue: Form Lost Data

**Solution**:
- Form auto-saves as you type (in browser memory)
- If you close browser, data may be lost
- Complete form in one session
- Do not refresh page before submitting
- If lost, restart from beginning

### Issue: Submission Failed

**Solution**:
- Check all required fields (marked with *)
- Verify all validations pass
- Ensure internet connection is stable
- Try submitting again
- Try different browser
- Contact support if persistent

### Issue: No Confirmation Email Received

**Solution**:
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address was entered correctly
- Wait 5-10 minutes for delivery
- Check email with admin if no receipt on screen
- Request manual confirmation

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: How long does it take to complete the form?**
A: Typically 15-25 minutes depending on how quickly you have your documents ready. Personal forms are usually faster than business forms.

**Q: Can I save my progress and come back later?**
A: Yes, the form auto-saves as you type. However, if you close the browser completely, you'll need to restart. Best practice: Complete it in one session.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: You can review everything on the final step before submitting. After submission, contact the bank to request corrections.

**Q: Is my personal information secure?**
A: Yes, all data is encrypted with SSL/TLS and stored in a secure database with access controls. The form never shares your data with third parties.

**Q: How do I know my application was received?**
A: You'll see a receipt screen immediately, and an email confirmation will be sent to your provided email address.

### Account & Payment Questions

**Q: What account types are available?**
A: Personal: Savings, Custody, Numbered, Cryptocurrency. Business: Business Savings, Business Custody, Business Cryptocurrency.

**Q: What are the account opening fees?**
A: €25,000 standard accounts, €50,000 for numbered accounts. Exact amount shown during Step 8.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: SWIFT international wire transfer (EUR/USD) or USDT cryptocurrency (TRC20). KTT/Telex not accepted.

**Q: What if my payment fails or is not verified?**
A: Contact support with your Application ID. Payment must be verified before account opening. Refunds issued if application declined.

**Q: Can I pay after submission?**
A: Payment can be made before or after submission, but account processing won't begin until payment is verified.

---

### Documentation Questions

**Q: What documents do I need for a personal account?**
A: Passport/ID, proof of residence, bank information, and source of funds documentation. Upload during the form.

**Q: What documents do I need for a business account?**
A: Company registration, director information, beneficial owner details, and business documentation. Upload during form.

**Q: Can I upload multiple documents?**
A: Form uploads one document per field. If multiple pages, you can (1) combine into single PDF, or (2) upload separately and note in the form.

**Q: What file formats are accepted?**
A: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP. Convert PDFs to images for upload.

**Q: What if my document is too large?**
A: Compress using online tools or phone camera settings. Aim for 2-5 MB files.

### Signature Questions

**Q: How do I sign the form?**
A: Step 9 includes a signature pad. Draw your signature with mouse/trackpad, or upload a clear photo of your signature.

**Q: Is my digital signature legally binding?**
A: Yes, when combined with the terms & conditions you agree to, it constitutes a binding digital signature under most jurisdictions.

**Q: Can I upload a signature image instead?**
A: Yes, upload a clear photo or scan of your actual handwritten signature. Must be legible and dated.
